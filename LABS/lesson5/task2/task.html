The factorial of a number is that number multiplied by each of it's predecessors until you reach 0.
For example 3!(read as 3 factorial) is 3x2x1 = 6.
5! is 5x4x3x2x1 = 120

You are to complete the definition of a function that computes the factorial of a given number.
